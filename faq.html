About Ayesa Dream Couture

Ayesa Dream Couture is a luxury couture label specialising in fairytale-inspired gowns and corset sets, where fantasy meets handcrafted artistry. Each piece is designed to make you feel magical, confident, and one of a kind.

Our designs are inspired by nature, emotions, and fantasy elements — butterflies, florals, pearls, and soft pastel dreams. Every piece is handcrafted with intricate detailing and couture-level finishing that you simply will not find elsewhere.

We primarily focus on made-to-order couture, but limited ready-to-wear pieces may be available during special drops. Visit our Shop to see what is currently available.

Custom Orders & Process

Fill out our Custom Couture Form on the website with your design vision, measurements, and occasion details. Our team will contact you to begin your personal design journey.

Our couture process moves through six stages: inspiration and concept discussion, sketch development, fabric and embellishment selection, measurements and fittings, handcrafting and detailing, and finally final finishing and delivery. Every step is deeply personal.

Standard custom orders take 4–8 weeks from the date of confirmed payment and measurements. Express timelines are available for an additional charge — please mention your event date in the order form and we will advise accordingly.

Sizing & Fit

You can refer to our Size Chart on the contact page, or choose custom sizing for a perfect fit. When between sizes, we recommend sizing up.

Yes — we specialise in custom-fit couture tailored exactly to your measurements. Simply provide your bust, waist, hip, shoulder, and height details in the order form and we will craft the piece to your body.

We ensure proper fittings throughout the process, but minor alterations may be needed after delivery. We will guide you through any adjustments required. For customers ordering remotely, providing precise measurements from the start minimises any fit issues.

Use a flexible measuring tape. Measure your bust at the fullest part, waist at the narrowest point, and hip at the widest point — usually 7–9 inches below the waist. We recommend having a friend assist for accuracy. Visit our measurement guide for detailed instructions.

Payments

We accept major payment methods including UPI, debit and credit cards, and international payments. Payment details will be shared upon confirmation of your order.

For couture orders, a 50% non-refundable deposit is required before work begins, with the remaining 50% due before dispatch. This ensures your piece receives our full attention from the very first step.

Returns & Cancellations

Since all pieces are made-to-order, we do not accept returns or exchanges on custom couture. For ready-to-wear items, returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery in original, unworn condition. Please review our Returns Policy for full details.

Care Instructions

Dry clean only. Store your piece in a breathable garment bag away from direct sunlight. Handle delicate embellishments — butterflies, pearls, crystals — with care and avoid snagging on jewellery or rough surfaces.

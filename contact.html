How to Take Your Measurements

Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape straight across your back.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline — the smallest part of your waist.

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, usually 7–9 inches below the waist.

For the most flawless fit, we recommend choosing our made-to-measure service.

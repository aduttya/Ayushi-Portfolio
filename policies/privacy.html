Policies

Privacy Policy

Effective: March 2026

Ayesa Dream Couture ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you place an order or contact us:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Delivery address and location
  • Body measurements submitted for custom orders
  • Order details, design preferences, and communications
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party providers — we do not store card details)

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfil your orders
  • To communicate with you about your order, design, and delivery
  • To improve our products and services
  • To send you updates or promotional content (only with your consent)

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with trusted service providers (e.g., courier companies, payment processors) solely for the purpose of fulfilling your order.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfil your order and comply with legal obligations. Measurement and design data for custom orders may be retained for up to 2 years to assist with future orders.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, please email us at hello@ayesadreamcouture.com.

Cookies

Our website may use minimal cookies to improve your browsing experience. No personal data is collected via cookies without your consent.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us at hello@ayesadreamcouture.com.

Design Process

From Dream to Dress

Every couture piece begins as a dream and transforms into a masterpiece through a detailed artistic journey.

01

Inspiration

Nature, butterflies, flowers, emotions, and fantasy worlds. The universe around us becomes the first sketch.

02

Moodboard Creation

Colors, textures, and visual storytelling come together into a curated world built around your vision.

03

Sketching

Hand-drawn couture sketches bringing imagination to form — each line a promise of what is to come.

04

Fabric Selection

Soft tulles, organza, silks, and delicate materials chosen with care for drape, texture, and feeling.

05

Embellishment Design

3D butterflies, pearls, crystals, and floral appliqués — every detail decided with intention and artistry.

06

Sampling

A first prototype is created and refined until every proportion and silhouette feels exactly right.

07

Final Creation

Handcrafted detailing and finishing — the most intimate stage, where patience becomes beauty.

08

The Final Look

A dream transformed into a wearable fairytale. You wear not just a garment, but an entire world.
